## Copywriting Frameworks Research (Kochfoto Business)

Jonah asked about copywriting frameworks for his photography business. He's planning an HTML email campaign to sell leather-bound bar/bat mitzvah albums to past clients.

### Key Frameworks Discussed:
- **AIDA** (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action) - the classic
- **PAS** (Problem, Agitate, Solution) - recommended for his use case since clients already have photos but need to be reminded what's at stake
- **BAB** (Before-After-Bridge) - good for showing transformation
- **FAB** (Features-Advantages-Benefits)
- **4 C's** (Clear, Concise, Compelling, Credible)
- **4 U's** (Useful, Urgent, Unique, Ultra-specific)

### Recommended Approach for Mitzvah Album Sales:
PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution) with AIDA opening:
- **Problem**: Photos sitting in digital limbo, buried in folders
- **Agitate**: Kids grow fast, hard drives crash, emotional urgency fades while regret grows
- **Solution**: Leather-bound album designed to last generations

Jonah will formulate more specific details (audience segments, price points, urgency hooks) before drafting the actual copy.
