## Minor / Court Appearance Requirement

In Maryland, minors may not be able to simply pay traffic citations online. Montgomery County may require:
- The minor (and a parent) to appear in court, **or**
- A parent to pay on the minor's behalf

**Action:** Call Montgomery County District Court at the number on the citation to confirm whether a court appearance is required or if a parent can pay directly.
